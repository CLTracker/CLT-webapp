The Paper Crisis

Conferences can spend a lot of money to print maps, agendas, marketing materials, and brochures. Imagine a tradeshow for 500 people. According to a study by North Central Carolina University, over 170 trees could be consumed. This amounts to 200 pounds of wood and over $750 in printing costs.

Too Much Paper

According to the same study by North Central Carolina University, attendees from the same tradeshow can end up carrying 10 pounds of paper. With so much paper, the message of exhibitors and organizers can become lost and end up in the trash can.
